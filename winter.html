OLGA.

Yes! To Moscow, and as soon as possible.

[CHEBUTIKIN and TUZENBACH laugh.]

IRINA.

I expect Andrey will become a professor, but still, he won't want to live here. Only poor Masha must go on living here.

OLGA.

Masha can come to Moscow every year, for the whole summer.

[MASHA is whistling gently.]
